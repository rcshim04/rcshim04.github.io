Final Project

ICS4UO - ISP (COVID Cashier)

A program being written for my ICS4UO final project. An original educational autoscroller game, focused on the 2019 Novel Coronavirus pandemic. Partner: Celeste Luo

Gameplay

In the game, the user plays the role of a cashier worker at a fast food restaurant. Quarantine restrictions have been enforced in the workplace and there are few workers in the facility. There are two levels, the learning/training level for the environment and the live level where the user is tested on what they learned. In the learning/training level, the worker will be placed in a safe restaurant environment with stations they are to visit. At each, they will learn about a precaution, why it's important, and how to implement it. In the live testing level, the worker will go through their job, from the start to the end of their shift. The goal is to not be infected by the virus, no matter what events the worker must face.
